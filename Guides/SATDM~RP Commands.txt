---------------------------------Business Help--------------------------------
Businesses are indicated on the map by the yellow circle icons and in the gameworld by a spinning blue house pickup.
Type /buybiz to purchase the business. Must be within 7-8m of business icon.
Type /sellbiz to sell your business. Must be within 7-8m of business icon.
Type /cashbox to transfer you business' earnings to your personal bank account. Must be within 7-8m of business icon.
Type /gotobiz to teleport to your biz

--------------------------------Teleport Help---------------------------------
/home will teleport your spawn point
/telebank will teleport you to the bank
/exitbank will teleport you to your location before teleporting to the bank
